My Mission In 2023

Mission

To ensure quality education, maintain sound academic environment and provide services at nonprofit basis in order to establish it as an Award Giving Institution.

Vission

*To enhance participation of students in business and technology.

*Provide a teaching, research, development and training institute for our promising younger people.

*Looking forward to some life oriented income generating; self-sustained activities where our younger generation can get involve educating themselves and upgrade their socioeconomic status.

Goals

*To develop and maintain proper physical infrastructure.

*To develop and maintain modern CSE, ECE and BBA Lab.

*Provide scholarship and financial supports to the brilliant and needy students.

*Organized extracurricular activities for students as like- club, seminar, exhibition, games and sports, cultural program, study tour, picnic etc.
